Each program is carefully designed to meet the developmental needs and learning styles of specific age groups.

Toddler Program

18 months - 3 years

A nurturing environment focused on early development, sensory exploration, and building foundational social skills.

Program Features

  • Sensory play activities
  • Language development
  • Motor skills development
  • Social interaction
  • Creative expression
  • Potty training support
