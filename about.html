Our Mission

At Mountolives Kiddies School, our mission is to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where every child can discover their potential, develop critical thinking skills, and grow into confident, compassionate individuals. We are committed to delivering high-quality education that prepares our students for future academic success while fostering creativity, character, and a lifelong love of learning.

Our Story

Learn about our journey and commitment to educational excellence

2010

Foundation

Founded as a small community initiative with a big dream: to create an educational environment where every child could thrive. Our founders, a group of passionate educators and parents, recognized the need for a school that would combine academic excellence with character development and creative expression.

2014

First Expansion

Grew from a modest 50-student school to serving over 200 families. Our growth was guided by our unwavering commitment to providing personalized attention, innovative teaching methods, and a supportive environment that celebrates each child's unique talents and abilities.

2018

New Campus

Moved to our current state-of-the-art facility with expanded classrooms, modern learning spaces, and enhanced outdoor play areas. This allowed us to serve more families while maintaining our high standards of education and care.

2024

Today & Beyond

Today, Mountolives Kiddies School stands as a beacon of educational excellence in our community. We have consistently achieved outstanding academic results while maintaining our focus on developing well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of tomorrow.
